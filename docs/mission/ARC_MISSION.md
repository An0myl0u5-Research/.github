# ARC Mission (Canonical)

An0m5-ARC — the **An0myl0u5 Research Collective** — is the open research layer of the wider An0myl0u5 network.

We are a decentralised research collective building infrastructure for **annotated, community-verified knowledge**.

Every contribution — whether a single word correction or a full research submission — is signed and attributed forever.

Our mission is to make research claims auditable, attribution durable, and peer review perpetual through transparent, versioned, and reproducible public artifacts.
